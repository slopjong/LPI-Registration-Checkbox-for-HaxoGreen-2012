FAQ

Q: Will there be daypasses?
A: Yes there will be daypasses at the price of 12 EUR per day. They will be sold on-site.

Q: Only 23 EUR for four days? Are you frigging nuts?
A: We sure are! And, btw. that even includes free breakfast from Friday to Sunday as well as a coffee flatrate.

Q: What if I cannot come after all? Can I get a refund?
A: We will give refunds up to the end of the early-bird deadline. After Mai 22nd, we cannot do that, since we will already have bought stuff for you.

Questions, doubts, problems? Contact us at camp[at]hackerspace[dot]lu!

SSL?

Prefer a secured connection?
You can use our SSL secured site but be advised that we use a certificate issued by the CACert Community.
Your browser might not recognize it as legitimate if you have not previously installed their root cert.
